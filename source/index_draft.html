Tatami Premium WordPress Theme

Tatami is a new Premium WordPress theme by Elmastudio with a beautiful, minimal theme design and lots of modern theme features. Tatami has a responsive, mobile-first, off-canvas layout so your website will look as awesome on mobile devices as it looks desktop screens. The theme has two sidebars, a footer widget area and an easy-to-use theme options page. The big thumbnail option makes it easy to feature your best posts, alternatively you have also two smaller thumbnail options.
